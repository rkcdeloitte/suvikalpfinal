PRISM – Post-Mining Repurposing and Implementation Strategy Models
A structured framework for mine repurposing from concept to implementation
PRISM is a structured framework designed to support identified post-mining repurposing opportunities by evaluating suitable implementation pathways, financial and revenue-generation models, and the implementation requirements, roles, and responsibilities of mine owners, developers, operators, and other stakeholders throughout the project lifecycle. Built around five integrated pillars, PRISM guides {{CAT_TITLE}} projects from concept to operation.
